Tips for Searching Candidates

You can easily search your entire list of applied candidates by using, or combining, the data fields below into your search string:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Job title they applied to
  • Job location they applied to
  • Previous job title
  • Previous company

Advanced Boolean Search

  • QUOTES match exact phrases:
  • "Jane Doe"
  • "registered nurse"
  • "project manager"
  • OR makes the next term optional (default):
  • John OR Jack Doe
  • retail OR cashier
  • "Los Angeles" OR "New York"
  • AND requires the next term:
  • "human resources" AND management
  • software AND java
  • professor AND PhD
  • NOT excludes the next term:
  • director NOT executive NOT vp
  • "project manager" NOT construction
  • driver NOT truck
